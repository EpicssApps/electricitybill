🗑️ Delete Account & Data

Electricity Bill at OneClick — by Epix Apps

⚠️ Deleting your account and data is permanent and cannot be undone. Please make sure you want to proceed before following these steps.

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from the Electricity Bill at OneClick app, you can do so directly from within the app by following the simple steps below.

📋 What data will be deleted?

When you delete your account and data, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • All saved bill reference numbers (Electricity, Gas, Water, Internet)
  • All bill history stored locally on your device
  • Your Google Drive cloud backup data
  • Your Google Account connection to this app
  • All personalized bill names and settings

📱 How to delete your data from the App

1

Open the App

Open the Electricity Bill at OneClick app on your Android device.

2

Go to the About Tab

Tap on the "About" tab at the bottom navigation bar of the app.

Bottom Navigation → About
3

Scroll Down

Scroll down to find the "Account & Data Management" section at the bottom of the screen.

4

Tap "Delete Account & Data"

Tap on the Delete Account & Data button. A warning dialog will appear explaining what data will be deleted.

Delete Account & Data
5

Confirm by Typing "DELETE"

To prevent accidental deletion, you will be asked to type "DELETE" in a text field and tap the confirm button.

6

Done! ✅

All your data (local + cloud) will be permanently deleted and your Google Account will be disconnected from the app.

⏰ Data Retention

Once you confirm the deletion:

  • Local data is deleted immediately from your device
  • Cloud backup is deleted immediately from Google Drive
  • Google Account access is revoked instantly
  • No data is retained after deletion

📧 Need Help?

If you are unable to delete your data from the app or have any questions, you can request data deletion by contacting us via email.
